Instructions for uploading file
  • Click on the Upload a file button
  • Write your name in the form that opens (Your ashoka.edu.in email will be recorded)
  • Choose the file to upload
  • Click on Upload File
  • A link will be displayed on the page. Copy it and paste it in the google form below.
  • Alternatively, you can skip the "Upload A File" Section and directly provide a link to the file.
