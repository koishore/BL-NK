BL!NK: Get notified in the blink of an eye!

About

BL!NK is a webapp intended for Ashokans (students, faculty alike) to reduce the flood of emails in everyones' inbox, without compromising the information we get by categorizing and summarizing emails into bullet updates

Just a few things to know before you use BL!NK:

  • This webapp was created as a part of our Human Computer Interaction coursework.
  • Any update takes around 15-20 minutes to show up on the webpage, so please patient and donot fret if something doesn't show up immediately.
  • If you have any queries or complaints, drop an email at koishore[dot]roy[at]ashoka[dot]edu[dot]in.
